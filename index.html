ABOUT TOM & US

We love cats

We have created a fictional website for cat lovers. A cat is a small pet animal. It has four short legs and a beautiful furry tail. Its body is covered wiht soft and silky hairs. Its claws and teeth are sharp. It has bright and greyish eyes, It can see even in the darkness.
Cats can be found all over the world. They are beautiful small creatures resembling the likes of a tiger. They live on the streets as well as in our houses. Cats can be shy in nature and can even take time while adjusting with the environment. One needs to be patient and deal with them with a lot of love and care.
